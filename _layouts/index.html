About this page
I love anime and manga! I've followed many different series week-by-week and by far the hardest part about being a weekly reader is the dreaded period of time when I know there are spoilers out there. I always wished there was a way to get a small taste of the chapter without actually getting spoiled, hence, I created this small site.

This is the MVP version of this page. There's a lot more in the roadmap including a mobile app if people find this valuable enough.

The page is hosted at Github Pages
